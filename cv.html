RESEARCH

VLSI Design and Implementation of On-Line Local Mean Decomposition (LMD) [June 2013 – present]

Independent Researcher, Advisor: Prof. Shao-Yi, Chien
· Developed a novel LMD algorithm with low latency, low computational loading and low power consumption for real-time on-line ECG signal noise removal, and published a paper at BioCAS 2014 held in Lausanne, Switzerland.
· Designed an on-line LMD processor with 2.28mw total power, 2.33mm2 core area, and 4.9ms computational latency for wearable biomedical applications, and published a paper (oral presentation) at EDSSC 2015 held in Singapore.
· Designed an on-line ensemble LMD processor with 3.72mw total power, 2.42mm2 core area, and 19.6ms computational latency for real-time continuous biomedical signal analysis, and submitted a paper to ISCAS 2016.
