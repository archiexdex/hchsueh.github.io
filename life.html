Life

MUSIC

I have been putting enormous effort on gaining diverse experiences in life. I found myself closely connected to different forms of art. Music, above all, is the one that has laid the most profound impact on me. Ten years of training on classical piano and four years on violin enable me to perceive melodic beauty and instill emotions into my musical performance. Obsessed with the idea of sharing the joy of music with people, I performed in various places, formally and informally, to one-time audiences and to people I care about. Taking the course Mozart further drove me to putting music into historical and cultural contexts and analyzed it thoroughly. In order to appreciate more the beauty of music, I will dig deeper into the understanding of the musicians I like and the relevant historical background.

Culinary Art

Culinary art is another form of art that touches my soul. I am obsessed with the quest of combing taste, texture, aroma and presentation on a single dish, and intrigued by busy kitchen scenes where cooks, either for survival or self-fulfillment, rush around amidst the steamy aisles, sizzling foods and chef’s shouting orders. The motivation first drove me to joining the Cake&Pastry Club at NTU for a whole year. (The club is formally known as NTUESC — NTU Egg Studying Club, since the study and practice on egg cookery is fundamental in baking pastry) Later, a couple of months of informal apprenticeship at Rebirth, a popular café&restaurant, empowered me with practical cooking skills. Another month working as a line cook in a paste house enriched my human experiences and made me appreciate more the importance of having empathy. Afterwards, looking for opportunities to try on different dishes, I started cooking meals for friends. The process of planning recipes and cooking steps, gathering ingredients at food markets, and carrying out the actual cooking of several dishes in a rather short period of time all by myself was extremely exhausting but super exciting. As for the joy of sharing food I cook with friends? Unforgettable.

The ten of us were fully sponsored to visit Google Campus and Applied Materials headquaters in Silicon Valley, Stanford University and University of California, Berkeley.
